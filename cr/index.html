Signature Experiences

Beach Trifecta

• Playa Carrillo — palm-lined crescent with picnic tables and mellow surf (costarica.org).
• Playa Sámara — lively hub with cafés, surf shacks, boutique shopping (TwoWeeksInCostaRica).
• Playa Barrigona — white sand + electric water for secluded afternoons (MyTanFeet). Arrange 4x4 + cooler.

Bluewater & Billfish

Winter trade winds push captains south to Carrillo, keeping marlin, sailfish, dorado, tuna, roosterfish, and cubera snapper on tap (FishCostaRica). Schedule one offshore day + one inshore light-tackle morning so everyone gets time on deck.

Water + Wildlife Safari

Samara Adventures’ Wildlife & Mangrove Kayak floats the Ora River to Camaronal, spotting monkeys, iguanas, crabs, and aquatic birds before beach cocktails. Combine with Natural Center Tours kayaking (WhatsApp +506 8352-7183) for a private departure.

Sunset Catamaran Social

Samara Adventures catamaran charters include hotel pickup, open bar, snorkeling at Isla Chora, chef-made bites, and seasonal dolphin/whale sightings. Block the entire boat (12 pax) for the family so music & menu are yours.

Camaronal Turtle Patrol

Camaronal Wildlife Refuge hosts Olive Ridley, Leatherback, Hawksbill, and Pacific Green turtles nesting most nights June–January (EnterCostaRica). Rangers guide nighttime visits only — wear dark clothing, no flashes, and book early.

Nosara’s Miss Sky Canopy

21 runs across 11 km of cables soaring above valleys, rivers, and waterfalls (GypsyCabNosara). Morning departures catch cooler temps; add a waterfall swim + lunch in Nosara before heading back for pool time.

Day Trips & After-Dark Magic

Bioluminescence in Paquera

Bahía Rica’s kayak tour launches near the Paquera ferry — every paddle stroke ignites neon-blue plankton and you can jump in for a glowing swim (bahiarica.com). Plan on a 90-minute drive each way.

Isla Chora Snorkel

Kayak or SUP 30 minutes from Sámara to a white-sand wildlife refuge with parrotfish, pufferfish, and calm reefs (samaraadventures.com/ocean-kayak-snorkel). Combine with a beach picnic.

Camaronal Night Patrol

Join refuge guides 6pm–late to witness hatchlings race to the sea. June–January sees nightly arrivals; July/August sits squarely in peak season (EnterCostaRica). Limited permits — reserve now.

Logistics, Weather & Tips

Getting There

• Liberia Intl (LIR) → villa: ~2h15 on paved Route 21/150 (per TwoWeeksInCostaRica).
• Arrange a 20-seat sprinter for arrivals + a backup 4x4 for grocery / surf runs.
• Nosara commuter flights (SANSA) land 40 min north if you want quick hops for day tours.

Weather Snapshot

Late July/early August = lush "green" season: mornings are sunny (highs 85 °F), humidity hovers ~70%, and brief golden-hour showers keep everything emerald. Pack light linens + quick-dry gear; plan outdoor excursions before 2 PM.

Money & Services

Most restaurants + tours accept cards; keep colones for roadside fruit + tips (10% service fee usually included, add 5% for great service). WhatsApp is the default for every vendor — save numbers now.
